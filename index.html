About the Symposium

The 7th International Symposium on Materials from Renewables (ISMR) brings together leading scientists, engineers, industry innovators, and students to explore the evolving field of sustainable materials. As global demand for environmentally responsible technologies continues to grow, renewable materials are playing an important role in advancing the circular economy and enabling a sustainable future.

ISMR will showcase the latest research and advances in materials derived from renewable resources. The symposium will feature cutting-edge developments in the design, synthesis, characterization, processing, and application of these materials, while addressing the scientific and technological challenges that remain.

The technical program will provide a vibrant forum for presenting new discoveries, exchanging ideas, and fostering collaborations among researchers from academia, national laboratories, and industry. By bringing together experts from diverse disciplines, ISMR aims to accelerate innovation and advance sustainable materials that combine high performance with environmental responsibility, economic viability, and circularity.
